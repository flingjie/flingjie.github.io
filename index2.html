    Published: 二 18 三月 2014
    By flingjie

    In Clojure.

    (defn recur-fac [n]
    (if (= 1 n)
        1
        (* n (recur-fac (dec n)))))

    通常，可以改写成尾递归的方式来避免消耗栈导致栈溢出，改写如下：

    (defn recur-fac [n]
    (letfn [(fac [c r]
                (if (= 0 c)
                    r
                    (fac (dec c) (* c r))))]
    (fac n 1)))
